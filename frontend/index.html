Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mosquito nets

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed within 2-4 hours depending on the number of windows. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes! We offer up to 10-year warranty on our premium nets and 5-year warranty on standard installations. All workmanship is guaranteed.

Can I install the nets myself?

While some magnetic nets are DIY-friendly, we recommend professional installation for optimal fit and durability. Our installation comes with a warranty.

How do I clean the mosquito nets?

Gently vacuum or wipe with a soft damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals. We provide specific cleaning instructions with each installation.
